A conversation with Siddhartha Guvaju

Scholars can make the painting readable again. Only someone who lives the tradition can say what Bunga Dya means today. Eight questions, asked and answered.

About Siddhartha Guvaju

Siddhartha Guvaju is a Newar deeply involved in the living traditions that surround Bunga Dya, among them the Rato Macchindranath chariot festival, the procession that carries the deity through the streets of the Kathmandu Valley each year.
